Project Site Plan

Site Name

Bloggie

I chose the name Bloggie, since it is a website about movie reviews and news. I feel that it is a name that suggests what is being made known on the website. It is a striking and easy to remember name.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Bloggie is to provide interesting and trending movie reviews that help users make informed decisions about which movies to watch. Create a community of film enthusiasts who appreciate thoughtful reviews and discussions.

Scenarios

  • What are the must-watch science fiction movies from the last few years?
  • Does Bloggie have a section where I can find reviews and recommendations for independent films?

Color Schema

The selected colors for the website are:

  • Light Blue (#f0f4f8): Used for the background to create a calm and inviting atmosphere.
  • Dark Gray (#333333): Used for the main text to ensure readability.
  • Green (#4CAF50): Used for the header, footer, and primary buttons to signify growth and nature.
  • Darker Green (#2E7D32): Used for headings to draw attention and maintain a natural feel.

Typography

The fonts selected for the website are:

  • Roboto: Used for body text to ensure readability and modernity.
  • Merriweather: Used for headings to add a touch of elegance and readability.
